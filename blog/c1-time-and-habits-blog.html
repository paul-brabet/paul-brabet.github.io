Time and Habits

22 February 2017

which timebox and habits techniques did you try?

In all honesty, I haven't had an opportunity to try anything new yet! Though, I have participated in mindfulness before, and I must say that I find it rewarding. It's not something I have a habit of doing regularly, but coincidentally I happened to have a stressful afternoon so I took 10 minutes today to listen to a guide from a mindfulness app.

There are some other habit techniques that I want to try, which I'll get to in a later question.

what did you find interesting?

I've heard about most of these techniques before, but it was interesting to hear an explanation behind it; e.g. giving yourself small rewards isn't necessarily about 'If a finish x I will get reward y' but an unconcious habit that forms as a result.

what worked for you?

Before taking 10 minutes for a mindfulness session I was quite irritable and couldn't focus. After the session I was much more calm - like the frustrations had been emptied out of the basket that is my head - and I could focus more easily on the tasks ahead.

which will you experiment further with?

I should make a habit of mindfuless and meditation, even if I'm already feeling great. I am curious to try out the small steps and small celebrations method. They look easy to implement!
