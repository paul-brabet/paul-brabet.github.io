Breathing space

12 March 2017

How did the 'process over product' concept affect the way you tackled the site redesign and rebuild?
I decided at a certain point that I could not update the entire website in the time that I had, so I decided to just complete the tasks given. I had learnt enough for the time being.
What did you think about meditation before reading chapter 2?
Reading about evidence on the benefits on meditation only confirms my experiences that I have had with it. However it is entirely another to form a habit of doing it. That's what I really need to do.
What new things have you learnt about meditation?
That the benefits extend beyond mental well being and an ability to focus, but meditation appears to also benefit memory retention and even the immune system. I do feel some caution about believing this straight off the bat because only isolated studies appear to have been cited, but I accept the idea that there will be some sort of cognitive or health benefit in meditation.
Did any of the suggested meditation techniques stand out to you?
Focusing on breath strikes me as a vital component. If I ever forget my place during meditation, or do not know what to do, I can always bring myself back to breathing.
Any other musings?
Just thinking that if I want to try my utmost to avoid sickness this year, I might as well consider meditation as one experimental tool.
